# Commercial licensing

SpinUP is [source-available under PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0](./LICENSE). Non-commercial use — personal projects, research, evaluation, internal non-production tools — is free.

**Commercial use requires a separate agreement.** That includes, but is not limited to:

- Running SpinUP to serve production workloads that generate revenue (directly or indirectly).
- Offering SpinUP as a hosted or managed service to third parties.
- Bundling SpinUP into a commercial product.
- Any use where the primary purpose is deriving business value.

## Getting a commercial license

Contact **michal@jaskolski.pro** to discuss terms. Typical questions we'll want to answer together:

- What's the intended deployment (single tenant / multi-tenant / edge)?
